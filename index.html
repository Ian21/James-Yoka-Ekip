James Yoka Ekip

The man behind this website is James Yoka Ekip, a professionally trained accountant and self-made businessman from the Western Highlands of Papua New Guinea. Born into the Elti-Penambi group of Mount Hagen, James Yoka Ekip was educated in Western Highlands, East New Britain and Morobe provinces. He was raised in a Christian family and remains a devout Catholic. His philanthropy work started with the construction of a road connecting Ruki and Kenta to Kagamuga airport. He is a patron of Hagen art and craft, Western highlands history, Mount Hagen Golf Club, Andakelka coalition-style alliance, and various cultural and sporting events. .

Christmas Message From James Yoka Ekip

Dear Friends and Family, As we approach the end of 2024 and look forward to welcoming 2025, I find myself reflecting deeply on the journey I have undertaken. From my humble beginnings in the village and celebrating my first Christmas at Mun Catholic Mission, to experiencing countless festive seasons, each Christmas has brought its own unique memories. Now, in 2024, I see the transformations in myself: from a small village boy to a man over 50. Many loved ones have passed on, peers have aged with me, and the children I once held in my arms are now parents themselves. Our surroundings have changed, too.

    The Website

    2024

    December

    This website was created in December 2024 to provide an avenue for readers to share ideas on how to address the common issues and problems that confront us in contemporary society.

    • Profiling our leaders and getting them to tell their stories
    • Civic education and public awareness
    • Bringing young people together

    It is essentially about harnessing potential to excel in all spheres of life. The emphasis is on youth. With wisdom and knowledge from our rich cultural heritage, we intend to build a better future for our children.
